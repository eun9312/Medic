About Medic


Medic is built to provide easy access to medical information for average non-expert users. Many people do not recognize their diseases in the early stage due to lack of medical knowledge. Even though some symptoms are quite obvious, many patients do not visit a doctor because of busy work, long distance, or insufficient money. Such late detection of diseases causes harder and less successful treatment for people who are not medical experts. Medic allows users to have easy access to medical information so that more people can detect their symptoms earlier and visit hospital for more successful treatment.
